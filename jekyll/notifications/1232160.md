      ASSIGNMENT OVERVIEW
      
      Assignment Title: 1232160 - EMISSION REDUCTIONS PROGRAM DOCUMENT (ERPD) COTE DIVOIRE
      
      Assignment Countries:
        - Cote d'Ivoire
      
      ASSIGNMENT DESCRIPTION
      
      The overall objective of the assignment is to support CIV in the preparation of its ERPD in compliance with the Methodological Framework for submission to the CFP. More specifically, the World Bank is seeking to hire a consulting firm to 1) conceptualize the ER Program, in particular regarding the intervention strategy and ER program activities, operational and financial planning, and benefit-sharing arrangements, taking into consideration the existing programs and partnership in the ER Program area; 2) support the coordination of and consultations with actors involved in ER Program activities; 3) lead the writing of the entire ERPD, coordinate contributions from other partners (e.g. on carbon accounting) and ensure consistency across the ERPD; 4) develop an innovative financial mechanism to engage stakeholders in the program area (e.g. companies, smallholder cooperatives) into the ER Program in conjunction with benefit-sharing arrangements
      
      FUNDING SOURCE
      
      The World Bank Group intends to finance the assignment / services described below under the following:
        - BANK BUDGET
        - Cote d'Ivoire FCPF ER-PD Development
      
      ELIGIBILITY
      
      Qualifications:
      	Scientific degree in a policy field related to REDD+ and a minimum of 8 years of relevant professional experience;
      	Demonstrated track record on the development of REDD+ operations;
      	Experience in the design of financial mechanisms / instruments; 
      	Experience in working with the private sector;
      	Experience regarding sustainable commodity production and supply chains, in particular for cocoa, rubber and palm oil;
      	Good understanding of the FCPF processes and FCPF Methodological Framework;
      	Action-oriented and demonstrated ability to deliver quality products on time;
      	Demonstrated ability to draft clearly and concisely;
      	Excellent writing and communication skills in French and English;
      	Experience in stakeholder interactions in dynamic contexts and in managing different interest groups.
      
      
      INDIVIDUAL / FIRM PROFILE
      The consultant will be a firm. 
      
      SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
      The World Bank Group now invites eligible firms to indicate their interest in providing the services.  Interested firms must provide information indicating that they are qualified to perform the services (brochures, description of similar assignments, experience in similar conditions, availability of appropriate skills among staff, etc. for firms; CV and cover letter for individuals).  Please note that the total size of all attachments should be less than 5MB.  Consultants may associate to enhance their qualifications.
      
      Interested firms are hereby invited to submit expressions of interest.
      
      Expressions of Interest should be submitted, in English, electronically through World Bank Group eConsultant2 (https://wbgeconsult2.worldbank.org/wbgec/index.html)
      
      NOTES
      Following this invitation for Expression of Interest, a shortlist of qualified firms will be formally invited to submit proposals. Shortlisting and selection will be subject to the availability of funding.
      
      Only those firms which have been shortlisted will receive notification. No debrief will be provided to firms which have not been shortlisted.
