      ASSIGNMENT DESCRIPTION
      
      The World Bank Group (WBG) provides support to public and private sector institutions to develop payments and related financial infrastructure. Within the context of support for financial sector development in selected South East Asian Countries, WBG aims to (i) improve access for factory workers, through employee benefit schemes, to consumer credit and other benefits and (ii) support development of short term inventory and distribution finance solutions for small retailers.
      The main objectives of these assignments are: 
      (i) to assist a company in the design and implementation of a robust and responsible credit policy, risk management framework and operations for salary based consumer lending. The company will also be assisted in developing a strategy to transfer consumer lending to a credit institution.
      (ii) to assist consumer goods companies, distributors and financial institutions to put in place sustainable service through which to provide short term credit and loyalty solutions to small retailers 
      
      KEY TASKS OF ASSIGNMENTS:
      - Review a banks organisation and  capabilities to identify fit with proposed lending operations and identify gaps/workplan
      - Assess high level investment, costs and skills required to develop and implement lending approach
      - Support bank staff with implementation (planning and operations)
      - Support bank in review of corporate partners process and business requirements
      - Support product and process design of the bank/service provider
      - Support development of an appropriate risk function, methodology and operations
      
      The WBG is now looking for a pool of candidates who have relevant experience in the field to submit their CVs for potential projects below.
      - Support implementation of  Distribution Chain Finance solution
      - Employee benefit scheme credit management review and operations design
