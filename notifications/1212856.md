      ASSIGNMENT DESCRIPTION
      
      The chosen vendor will be responsible for the following tasks under the supervision of two units at the WBG: Operations, Policy, and Country Services (OPCS) and LLI:
       Identifying the skills gap of the frontline operational staff in the World Banks Russia Country Office in pitching RAS to clients. 
       Design, beta-testing, and delivery of one day training on pitching RAS that provides frontline operational staff with the key tools and skills needed to effectively sell RAS to clients. The learning should addresses key challenges faced by staff and cover a range of competencies, from building rapport with clients and active listening to closing a RAS deal with the client. The learning design should be experiential and context-specific, maximizing interactivity and application of skills. The vendor will develop a one-day training and all required materials.
